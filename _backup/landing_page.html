Myopia vs. Hyperopia

Most vision problems stem from where light focuses in your eye. VisionGuard helps reduce the strain that accelerates these conditions.

Myopia (Nearsightedness)

Light focuses in front of the retina. Objects far away look blurry. This is the #1 issue for screen users.

Hyperopia (Farsightedness)

Light focuses behind the retina. Close objects (like phones) look blurry, causing massive eye strain.
